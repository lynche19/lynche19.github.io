Adding Residents

 

Click on the Administrator’s Menu.

Choose “Resident Management”

To Add one resident click on the Green Plus Sign at the top of the page.  

 

 

1. Put in the Resident’s First Name

2. Put in the Resident’s Last Name

3. Choose the unit that the resident is living on.

4. Document the room number that the resident is staying in.

5. Document the Medical Record number and please note that the medical record number is the information that will be used to filter out duplicates.

6. Place the Admission date in the format of - mm-dd-yyyy (You cannot use any other format.

 7. The software will default to “In-house”.  If the resident goes into the hospital, you can change the default to NOT in-house. 

8.  Once all of the fields are full you click “OK” and your information will be saved.

Click on “Browse” to find the .csv file on your computer that you wish to upload.

Once the file appears on the “Browse” line, click “Upload”

Your files will upload and when the files are accepted the information will appear on the screen:

Make sure that the number of residents you had on your list is the number of residents that are accepted.  It is set up to evaluate two lines more than what you have listed, therefore it gives automatic warnings on the last two empty lines.  Disregard the warnings if your list appears.

 

 

When you click “OK” on this screen you will then return to the Resident Management – List of Residents screen as follows:

You will see the new resident’s you added.

Please note the icons to the right of the resident’s name. 

Explanation of Icons:

 

Edit resident information = Pencil

When you click on this icon you can go into the Resident specific information and make any changes.

Green Status Button Icon

When the resident is transferred to the hospital, you can click on this and change it to the color red which indicates the resident is not in the facility.  This will turn off the ability for staff to document on the resident.

 

 

Calendar Icon

When you click on the Calendar icon it will reveal the opportunity to put into the system the Assessment Reference Date for each resident. 

Once you put in the Assessment Reference Date for this particular resident the Resident List will reveal that the resident is in the lookback period by changing the color of the font displaying the resident’s name.

Please note that in this example above, Cory Aldrich and John Adams are in their lookback period.

Discharge Resident with no anticipation of Return Icon

The Red “X” displayed on the Resident Management Screen is for discharging the Resident with no anticipation of return.  Once you click on the “X” the software will ask you if the resident is being discharged with no return anticipated.  If they are not planning on returning you will answer “yes”.  If you do anticipate the resident to return, you will place the resident’s stay on hold.  To do this please refer to the instructions above labeled, “Green Status Button Icon”.
